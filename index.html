Why Should You Use RepFindr?

If you are like us, you've probably spent much of your adult life oblivious to the comings and goings of your elected representatives. Who is your Congressman? Who are your Senators? Your Mayor? Governor?

RepFindr will help you track down your elected officials, find their contact info, and see if their voting records match up with your interests.
